What is Bay Max?

    BayMax is a robot created by our team for the purpose of helping someone. This robot was inspired by BayMax from the movie Big Hero 6 where BayMax is an inflattable robot who serves as a personal healthcare companion. BayMax’s screen will provide a website with information and tools to assist the person using it. BayMax’s main purpose is to inform others and help people dealing with mental and health problems.
